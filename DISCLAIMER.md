# Disclaimer and Inclusion Criteria

## What this notebook is

A personal knowledge notebook and navigation tool — not a verified database, not an endorsement directory, not a comprehensive map.

Built by a researcher and Claude (Anthropic AI) from publicly available sources on the internet. Not independently fact-checked. Direct links are provided wherever possible so you can verify information at the source.

**Use this as a launchpad, not a landing place.** Follow the links. Talk to the organisations directly. Verify before you act.

---

## What inclusion means

Every organisation, mechanism, or pathway in this notebook was included because it met at least one of the following criteria at time of research:

| Criteria | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Health equity** | Active programs or investment targeting First Nations or broader health equity outcomes |
| **Education** | Investment in knowledge, capability, or workforce development at community level |
| **Community empowerment** | Anything that increases community agency, structural capacity, or financial independence — including legal empowerment, Indigenous-controlled capital, cooperative models, data sovereignty, political voice, and economic participation |

---

## What inclusion does not mean

- It is not a recommendation to work with, invest in, or partner with any listed organisation
- It is not a verification of any organisation's claims, impact figures, or financial performance
- It is not an assessment of any organisation's cultural safety practices, governance, or values alignment
- It is not a complete or exhaustive list of organisations working in this space
